“Through the practice of yoga, we are able to recognize our potential and tap into it.”
— B.K.S. Iyengar

My classes are inclusive, thoughtful, and accessible to all — regardless of age, fitness level, or experience. Classes are guided with precision and clarity, emphasizing safe alignment and the mindful use of props to support your unique body.

Whether you're recovering from injury, building strength, or simply seeking stillness, my approach helps you connect deeply with each posture. Iyengar Yoga offers the time and attention needed to develop understanding and confidence — making it ideal for beginners and long-time practitioners alike.
